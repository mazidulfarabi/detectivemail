Detective Mr. Alexander Ivan
Russian Crimes & Forensics Branch

Intelligent, observant, serious. Used to deductions and unravelling crimes. Has grown the popularity of being the living Sherlock Holmes. Knows perfect English despite being a Russian. Has a great knowledge of firearms.

Detective Kevin Miles
New York Police Department

Funny, intelligent, young. Has a great mind but doesnt really use it in deductions. Great at finding out new reasons for what might have been. Has a lot of ideas on modern technologies and devices. Good at reading people.
